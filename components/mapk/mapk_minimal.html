MAPK (minimal)¶

This passage was processed and assembled automatically by INDRA:

RAF, bound to RAF, phosphorylates MEK1 at Ser218 and Ser222. MEK1, phosphorylated at Ser218 and Ser222, is activated. Active MEK1 phosphorylate ERK2 at Tyr187 and Thr185. PP2A-alpha dephosphorylates MEK1 at Ser218 and Ser222. DUSP6 dephosphorylates ERK2 at Tyr187 and Thr185.
